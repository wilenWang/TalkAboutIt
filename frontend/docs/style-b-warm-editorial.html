Discussion AI 会取代程序员吗？
🚀
Elon Musk Tesla · SpaceX
AI won't replace programmers — it will 10x them. The real question isn't substitution, it's amplification. A single engineer with strong AI tooling will do the work of ten. But the creative vision, the taste, the ability to ask the right questions — that remains deeply human. What will change is the nature of the job itself.
Round 1 · 1/3
🧘
Naval Ravikant AngelList · Philosopher
Coding is leverage. AI is meta-leverage — leverage on leverage. The programmer who learns to wield AI becomes a force multiplier. But here's the nuance: the craft of programming — the deep satisfaction of building something from first principles — that's not going anywhere. What disappears is the boilerplate, the busywork, the mechanical translation of intent to syntax. That was never the valuable part anyway.
Round 1 · 2/3
🍎
Steve Jobs Apple Co-founder · Visionary
Technology is nothing without the humanities. The best programmers have always been the ones who sit at the intersection of technology and liberal arts. AI doesn't change that equation — it intensifies it. The future belongs to those who can taste. Who can feel, in their gut, whether something is beautiful or not. An AI can generate a thousand variations, but it cannot tell you which one matters. That requires a human soul.
